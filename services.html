When you look at the comparison between renting a space and buying one, the benefit is in your increased rights as an owner. You will never have to worry about the rent of your space increasing, suddenly being taken away, or excessive rules and restrictions implied a landlord. The space is yours to customize it any way you like so that you can get maximum enjoyment out of your investment. Renters are constantly faced with the prospect that their rent will escalate in the future due to rising building costs. As mortgage payments should remain relatively stable over time, Luxury Warehouse owners can avoid these problems.
